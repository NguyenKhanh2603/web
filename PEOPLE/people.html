Principal Investigator

Assoc. Prof.
Quan Thanh Tho
(Quản Thành Thơ)

Dr. Quan Thanh Tho is an Associate Professor in the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam. He obtained his B.Eng. in Information Technology from HCMUT in 1998 and completed his Ph.D. at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2006.

His research interests span Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, intelligent systems, and formal methods. He currently serves as the Dean of the Faculty.

Visit his official home page at: https://www.cse.hcmut.edu.vn/qttho/
